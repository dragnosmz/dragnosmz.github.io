I've always believed that social media is useless.  
The way that people act on these platforms is kind of more important than what they are talking about.   
Most of these platforms are exclusively for short-form content.
It makes it nearly impossible to publish anything meaningful on them.   

I can't stand it when people pretend to be a victim of their own behavior, although they aren't the ones in control.    

*why is it important to learn to be bored?*  

Because all of these things that you are addicted to are hyper-stimulating to your brain.
You only need them because your standard for what entertains you is extremely high.  
People don't have the attention span anymore to just go out in the middle of nowhere and enjoy life.  


A run with a good audiobook takes me down from the insanity of the internet, and reminds me how beautiful this life is.
