0x05 lost while searching You

2023-05-20

Deep within my heart, a longing stirs, yearning for a connection that remains elusive. It is a desire to be with you, a girl who has captured my thoughts and ignited a flame within my soul. Though our paths have yet to cross, the mere idea of you fills my days with hope and anticipation.

In my mind, I paint pictures of your radiant smile, imagining the joy it would bring to my life. I envision the sound of your laughter, a melody that would resonate in my ears and warm my spirit.
You are the embodiment of my dreams, a presence that I crave, even though we have not yet met.
I remain steadfast in my desire to find you, to know you, and to create cherished memories together.

Until that moment arrives, I will continue to hold onto the anticipation of what the future may hold, eager to embark on a journey of love and companionship.
In this unspoken bond, I discover the strength to pursue my dreams and embrace the beauty of the unknown.

-A seeker enthralled by the mystery of you
